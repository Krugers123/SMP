# Disclaimer

SMP - Semantic Multiplex Protocol is an experimental open-source protocol for structured human-AI communication.

It is not a guarantee of safety, truth, alignment, legal compliance, medical correctness, financial suitability, or security.

SMP packets can help carry intent, context, risk, and boundaries, but downstream AI systems may still misunderstand, ignore, transform, or incorrectly apply that information.

For consequential domains, human review, professional judgment, applicable law, and local governance remain necessary.
